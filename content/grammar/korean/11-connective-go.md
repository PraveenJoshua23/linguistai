---
id: connective-go
title: Connective -고 (and / then)
level: Intermediate
summary: '-고 connects two actions or clauses in sequence, meaning “and then”.'
examples:
  - text: 아침에 일어나고 커피를 마셨어요.
    romanization: Achime ilonago keopireul masyeosseoyo.
    translation: I woke up in the morning and drank coffee.
  - text: 친구를 만나고 영화를 봤어요.
    romanization: Chingureul mannago yeonghwareul bwasseoyo.
    translation: I met a friend and watched a movie.
  - text: 숙제를 끝내고 집에 갔어요.
    romanization: Sukjereul kkeunnaego jibe gasseoyo.
    translation: I finished the homework and went home.

---
Place -고 after the verb stem to link it with the next action. It shows that the second action happens after the first. The two clauses share the same subject unless otherwise marked. Use -고 in informal and formal speech alike.
