---
id: honorifics
title: Honorifics -(으)시-
level: Intermediate
summary: '-(으)시- is added to verb stems to honorify the subject of the sentence.'
examples:
  - text: 할머니께서 차를 ___ 마셨어요.
    romanization: Halmeoni-kkeseo cha-reul ___ maseosseoyo.
    translation: Grandmother drank the tea.
  - text: 선생님이 회의에서 ___ 발표하셨습니다.
    romanization: Seonsaengnim-i hoeui-eseo ___ balpyohasyeotseumnida.
    translation: The teacher gave a presentation at the meeting.
  - text: 아버지가 집에 ___ 돌아오셨다.
    romanization: Abeoji-ga jib-e ___ doraoshyeotda.
    translation: Father returned home.

---
Attach -(으)시- directly after the verb stem when the subject is someone you respect. It works with all verb endings, including -아요/어요 and -습니다. The honorific does not change the meaning of the verb, only shows respect.
