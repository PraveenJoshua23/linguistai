---
id: possessive-no
title: Possessive の
level: Beginner
summary: 'の links two nouns, showing possession or relationship (A の B = B of A).'
examples:
  - text: これは私の本です。
    romanization: kore wa watashi no hon desu.
    translation: This is my book.
  - text: 田中さんの車です。
    romanization: Tanaka-san no kuruma desu.
    translation: It is Mr. Tanaka’s car.
  - text: 東京の天気はいいです。
    romanization: Tōkyō no tenki wa ii desu.
    translation: Tokyo’s weather is good.

    answerIndex: 0

---
の connects nouns: the first noun modifies the second. It shows possession (私の本 = my book), origin, or type (日本語の先生 = Japanese teacher). The pattern is A の B.
