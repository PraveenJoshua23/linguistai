---
id: te-form
title: The て-form
level: Intermediate
summary: The て-form connects actions and makes polite requests with -てください.
examples:
  - text: 本を読んで、寝ました。
    romanization: 'Hon o yonde, nemashita.'
    translation: I read a book and then slept.
  - text: 窓を開けてください。
    romanization: Mado o akete kudasai.
    translation: Please open the window.
  - text: 音楽を聴いて、料理します。
    romanization: 'Ongaku o kiite, ryouri shimasu.'
    translation: I listen to music while cooking.
exercises:
  - prompt: 朝ごはんを食べ___ください。
    promptTranslation: Please ___ breakfast.
    options:
      - て
      - た
      - で
      - に
    answerIndex: 0
    explanation: The verb 食べ needs the て-form 食べて before ください.
  - prompt: 宿題を終わら___、テレビを見ます。
    promptTranslation: 'After finishing homework, I watch TV.'
    options:
      - し
      - しで
      - せ
      - せて
    answerIndex: 3
    explanation: The verb 終わる becomes 終わらせて to link actions.
  - prompt: 駅まで歩い___。
    promptTranslation: I walk to the station.
    options:
      - て
      - で
      - た
      - に
    answerIndex: 0
    explanation: 歩く changes to 歩いて to connect to the following clause or end the sentence.
  - prompt: 友達に手紙を書___、郵便局へ行きました。
    promptTranslation: I wrote a letter to a friend and went to the post office.
    options:
      - いて
      - いてで
      - いで
      - い
    answerIndex: 0
    explanation: 書く becomes 書いて to link the two actions.
---
The て-form is made by changing the verb ending to て (or で). It can link two actions, showing sequence or simultaneity. Adding ください after the て-form makes a polite request. Use it after the verb stem, not after a noun.
